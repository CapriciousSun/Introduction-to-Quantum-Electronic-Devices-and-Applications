\noindent Evaluate the dipole matrix element $\mathcal{P} = q\braket{\psi_{210} | z | \psi_{100}}$ for the specified transition. Leave your answer in terms of the Bohr radius $a_{0}$ and the electron charge $q$. 

\color{violet}
This is an allowed energy transition, so the energy transition would have the form
\begin{gather*}
\braket{\psi_{210} | z | \psi_{100}} = \begin{pmatrix}
H_{100, 100} & H_{100, 210} \\
H_{210, 100} & H_{210, 210}
\end{pmatrix} = \begin{pmatrix}
0 & H_{100, 210} \\
H_{210, 100} & 0
\end{pmatrix} = \braket{\psi_{210} | H' | \psi_{100}}
\end{gather*}
