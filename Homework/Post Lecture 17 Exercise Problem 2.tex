\noindent If the $N$ lattice atoms can each contribute thermal energy $E_{T} = \frac{1}{2}k_{B}T$ per degree of freedom to the coherent state, how much total energy can the lattice contribute. 

\color{violet}
If there are $N$ lattice atoms each contributing $\frac{1}{2}k_{B}T$ of energy, then total thermal energy for 2 degrees of freedom is
\begin{gather*}
E_{T} = 2 * N * \frac{1}{2}k_{B}T = Nk_{B}T
\end{gather*}
