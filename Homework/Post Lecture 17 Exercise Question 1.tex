\noindent If all $N / 2$ Cooper pairs are part fo the coherent state and the energy gap for each Cooper pair is $\Delta$, what is the energy gap for the total coherent state?

\color{violet}
If $N ~ 10^{22}$ and $\Delta ~ 10^{-4}$, then the energy gap for the total coherent state is
\begin{gather*}
E = \frac{N}{2}\Delta
\end{gather*}
\color{black}
